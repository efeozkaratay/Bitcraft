Bitcraft Settlement Planner

  • This tool includes 10,000 hexes for planning your settlement layout.
  • You can zoom in/out by mouse wheel or by zoom buttons on left panel.
  • You can pan the map by holding left mouse button on the map.
  • You can save your map by clicking Save Plan button. This will save a .json file for you. You must save your plan before exit.
  • You can load your saved plans or plans shared with you by others. Click Load Plan button and select .json file of the plan you wish to see.
  • Select a color from left panel and click on hex you wish to color. You can find blueprints for structures in Building Plans.
  • Last option of color on panel is for claim borders. Select that if you also like to add your claim borders for a better layout.
  • You can also add text to plan by double clicking on any hex. This will open a text box, enter your text and hit Enter on your keyboard.
  • Reset button will reset everything on your plan. No way to revert this, so use it carefully.
  • Good luck planning and building your settlement fellow Bitcrafters!
